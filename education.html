Intro to Information Systems

BMGT301

Prior to this class, I didn't fully understand what differentiated Information Systems from other similar fields. I chose this major because I knew that it laid somewhere on the intersection of Business and Technology. During this class I explored the true potential of a degree in IS. I learned how companies could create a sustainable competitive advantage by leveraging technology. I also learned the importance that big data and technology have when trying to capture consumers, differentiate offerings, and streamline customer experience. While covering these topics, I also completed coursework in Excel and worked towards a certification. After finishing top of my class, I was offered a position as a TA and was given the opportunity to be a leader and mentor to other IS students.

Skills: Excel

Pocketbook Politics: A History of American Buying and Selling

HIST289R

Originally, I enrolled in this class to fulfill a general education requirement but I soon became deeply engrossed in dissecting American consumerism. During the semester we looked at how consumerism progressed over time, and how the US evolved from a colonial society to a global consumer society. In this class I studied the political aspects of consumerism and the power the American public has. For my capstone I explored the impact that consumers had on the success of the civil rights movement.

Relevant Course Work

Strategy Consulting

B3MIN1022

The Dutch education system stood out from that of the US in that all classes were case and application based. While completing my minor in consulting I was given the opportunity to directly apply what we learned in the classroom to real business problems. For our capstone project we worked with a team to find a local company, diagnose a strategic issue they had, and propose a solution. We partnered with a bubble tea shop and were given access to our clients financial, sales, and product data. With this data and other research, we were able to conduct a competitive analysis and create an actionable plan for them to increase differentiation and capture a larger market share.
